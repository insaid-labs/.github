In this organization, you will find the **source code** and infrastructure needed to deploy the machines we publish on platforms like TryHackMe. Our goal is to share how vulnerabilities are designed from scratch.


Here we document the full lifecycle of a CTF machine:

* **Dockerfiles & Compose:** Containers with vulnerable services ready to deploy.
* **System Configurations:** Bash/Python scripts to provision VMs.
* **Snapshots & OVAs:** Links to full machine images.
